Research

I am Professor of Computer Science at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M). I do research on computer security and privacy. I am teaching three courses in 2019-20: the undergraduate course in Security Engineering and the graduate courses on Malware Analysis and Cybercrime, cyberterrorism and cyberwar.
