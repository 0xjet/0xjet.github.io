Research

I am Professor of Computer Science at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M). I do research on computer security and privacy. I am teaching three courses in 2020-21: the undergraduate course in Security Engineering and the graduate courses in Malware Analysis and Cybercrime.
