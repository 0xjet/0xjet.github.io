Open PhD Position

UC3M’s Computer Security (COSEC) group invites applications from highly motivated candidates for a fully funded Ph.D. position in the area of cybersecurity for mobile and IoT devices. The successful candidate will work under the supervision of Prof. Juan Tapiador at UC3M and Prof. Narseo Vallina at the IMDEA Networks Institute.

What is funded

The Ph.D. position at UC3M is fully covered (€1.600 gross per month). This salary includes social and health benefits. The group also provides funding for registration and travel expenses to attend industrial and academic conferences in the area. At least one fully-funded internship abroad is expected during the Ph.D. The payment is made on a monthly basis.

Duration

The contract will be for one year, renewable based on performance.

Eligibility

Candidates must hold a M.Sc. in areas related to computer science or telecommunications engineering, or being currently enrolled in one. The candidate must have a high command of English, both verbal and written, and will work in a highly international environment. The candidate must be ready to join UC3M as soon as possible, and no later than April 1, 2021.

Either software analysis or data analysis skills (e.g., traffic analysis) are recommended, as well as coding skills (mainly Java and Python) and an interest for working in the area of cybersecurity while conducting practical research. Good presentation skills will be a plus.

How to apply

Applicants interested in the position should submit their application here (call code 2021/049). The deadline for applications is February 24, 2021. Review of applications will begin immediately.

For enquiries about the position, please contact by email Juan Tapiador or Narseo Vallina.
